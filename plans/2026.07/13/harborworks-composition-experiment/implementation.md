## Deliberate deviations

- No durable specification was created before experimentation because the
  maintainer explicitly deferred it until the manifest semantics are clearer.
- No implementation was promoted into committed `src/`; the experiment stays
  on the `.cache/src` side of the code-promotion ladder.
- Fixture revisions are labels rather than nested Git repositories so the
  investigation can preserve inputs without committing nested `.git` state.
